Privacy Policy for Shopee Copy Product

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Overview

Shopee Copy Product is a Chrome extension that helps sellers quickly extract product information (title, price, description) from Shopee Taiwan product pages and download product images and videos. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

Data We DO Collect

  • Shopee product page data: Product title, price, description, image URLs, and video URLs — processed only when you actively click the extension icon
  • User preferences: Extension settings stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Browsing History: We do not track your browsing activities outside of Shopee product pages
  • Login Credentials: We do not collect or store your Shopee username, password, or session tokens
  • Usage Analytics: We do not collect usage statistics, telemetry, or error reports
  • Payment Information: We do not collect or process any payment data

How We Use Information

Local Processing Only

  • All product data extraction is performed locally on your device within the browser
  • Product data is displayed in the extension popup and copied to your clipboard only when you click the copy button
  • Images and videos are downloaded directly from Shopee CDN to your local downloads folder
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services

Clipboard Access

  • The extension requests clipboardWrite permission to copy product data to your clipboard
  • The extension requests clipboardRead permission to read JSON data from your clipboard when filling seller edit page forms
  • Clipboard data is never transmitted externally

Data Sharing

We DO NOT Share Data With:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers
  • Any external parties or servers

Website Communication

  • The extension communicates directly with shopee.tw and seller.shopee.tw to read product page content and fill seller forms
  • The extension fetches images from img.susercontent.com for download purposes
  • These communications are standard browser requests and do not involve our servers

Data Security

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • The extension requires explicit user action (clicking the icon) before any data extraction begins
  • All communications with Shopee use HTTPS encryption

Permissions Requested

PermissionPurpose
activeTabAccess the current Shopee product page to extract data
clipboardWriteCopy product data to your clipboard
clipboardReadRead clipboard data for filling seller edit forms
contextMenusRight-click context menu to save images as JPG
downloadsDownload product images and videos to your computer
host_permissionsAccess shopee.tw, seller.shopee.tw, and img.susercontent.com for data extraction and media download

User Control

  • You can disable or remove the extension at any time via chrome://extensions
  • No stored data persists after extension removal
  • The extension only activates when you click its icon on a Shopee product page

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.

This privacy policy applies to the Shopee Copy Product Chrome extension.
